Product Design: DC Central Kitchen

At Berkeley, I joined Blueprint, a student organization dedicated to building technology for non-profits. I'm working on product design for a customer loyalty mobile app for DC Central Kitchen, a non-profit striving to make fresh and healthy food accessible and affordable for victims of food insecurity in Washington D.C.'s food deserts.

Early mid-fidelity prototypes in Figma.

Graphic Design: Jacobs Institute for Design Innovation

I'm working as a graphic designer for the UC Berkeley Jacobs Institute for Design Innovation. I've been designing and producing outreach and communication materials. Recently, I designed the branding for the 2019 Jacobs Winter Design Showcase.
